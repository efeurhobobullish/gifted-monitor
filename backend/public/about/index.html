✦ Our Story

About Gifted Monitor

We built the tool we always wished existed — fast, simple uptime monitoring that tells you on WhatsApp, not email.

Who We Are

Gifted Monitor is a product of Gifted Tech — a software development studio focused on building practical, well-crafted tools for developers and businesses. We're a small team with a big belief: that monitoring your infrastructure shouldn't require an enterprise contract or a PhD in DevOps.

Why We Built This

We've been on the receiving end of angry customer messages: "Your site's been down for two hours — didn't you know?" It's embarrassing. It's avoidable. Email alerts get buried. SMS costs money. App push notifications need a phone with internet. But WhatsApp? Everyone reads WhatsApp immediately.

That's the insight behind Gifted Monitor. We built a monitoring tool that sends alerts where you'll actually see them — instantly — so you can act before users even notice.

What We Monitor

  • HTTP/HTTPS websites and APIs
  • Custom request paths with configurable methods (GET, POST, HEAD)
  • Response time and status codes
  • Uptime percentage over the last 30 checks
  • Down & recovery events with instant WhatsApp notifications

Our Values

  • Reliability first — our monitoring runs every few minutes, no gaps.
  • Privacy by design — we store only what we need, nothing more.
  • Speed matters — alerts go out the moment something changes.
  • Simple to use — no configuration files, no infrastructure setup.
